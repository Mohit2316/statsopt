Service Providers

Statsopt is a Silicon Valley product company. We partner with select firms to extend our reach and meet customer demand for additional services such as customized trainings, systems administration, and analytical support.

Praescient Analytics

Praescient Analytics is a mission-focused team of analysts and engineers delivering comprehensive solutions to clients across a range of industries. Composed mainly of U.S. veterans, Praescient was founded in 2011 with the goal of providing best-of-breed consultants who truly understand data, analysis, and technology. In partnership with Statsopt, Praescient has a proven track record in the following services:

  • Training
  • CONUS and OCONUS Embedded Analysis Support
  • Statsopt SME Analysis Support
  • System Administration
  • Data Integration & ETL
  • Plug-In & Helper Development

For more information on Praescient Analytics, please contact info@praescientanalytics.com

PVM

PVM's mission is to provide systems engineering and software lifecycle support to Statsopt deployments. PVM was founded in 2010 and has decades of experience supporting large-scale mission essential software systems. PVM is recognized for providing quality customer support and innovative solutions for the Statsopt platform.

For more information on PVM, please contact info@patvmackinc.com

SHINE Systems & Technologies

SHINE Systems & Technologies (SHINE) offers tailored solutions to government and commercial customers. Their approach is to mitigate risk and eliminate threats by aligning senior analysts with mission objectives to achieve success throughout the business life cycle. Although aided by powerful technology, analysts ultimately rely on their own instincts and intellect. SHINE understands the need for exceptional people and collaborates with their customers to provide the process and strategies required for every crucial mission.

Statsopt Core Capabilities include:

  • Statsopt Field Service Representatives servicing CONUS and OCONUS mission areas including: Counter-Insurgency, Counter-Terrorism, Counter-Narcotics, Targeting, and Threat Finance
  • Cyber Security
  • Business Intelligence including Fraud Detection
  • Statsopt Training

For more information on SHINE Systems & Technologies, please contact info@shinesystech.com.
