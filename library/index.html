Impact Studies

Impact studies show how our products have solved critical problems for government agencies, financial service institutions, and non-profit organizations.

Technical Triumphs in Transaction Data
In 2013, a major credit card transaction company that processes more than 60 billion transactions per year approached us about modernizing their data infrastructure.
Fighting Child Pornography

Finding Missing and Exploited Children

Coordinating Response Efforts After Hurricane Sandy

Fielding an Advanced Analytic Capability in a War Zone
